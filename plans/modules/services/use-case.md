Use case 1. 

- Templates and work orders both have a service items child table
- In either record view users click the drop down to select a service or leave it as custom
- Once a service is selected the price of the service from the real service row carries over to the row it was linked to.
- The price remains editable in the child tables of work orders and services but the actually row in the service table doesnt change.
- Service items added to a work order and template effect the customers cost AND Company expenses.
