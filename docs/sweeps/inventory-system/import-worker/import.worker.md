only workers create inventory. from the staged row the worker takes the import -  product - item number -  dye lot- warehouse - location, starting stock - cost -freight - notes, and they take those fields and they slap them into an inventory row. The worker also gets the cost per unit-  freight per unit - category slug + unit of measures & abreviations - . and most importantly the coverage per unit from the product. only products with the 4 special categories accept and require coverage per units, the worker takes it per inv product, inventory rows calculate the coverage balance with it. what remains editable in the inventory is the item # - Dye lot - Warehouse - Location - isarchived boolean - notes. cut logs are out of scope but cut logs calculate the total cut sum and the inventory domain calculates (starting stock minus cut sum = stock balance) (coverage balance = stock balance * coverage per unit)
